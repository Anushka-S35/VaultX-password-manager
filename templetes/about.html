About VaultX

Problem: Students reuse similar passwords on many sites. If one password is leaked, many accounts can be compromised.

Solution: VaultX lets students store and retrieve passwords securely. For this demo, data is stored locally. Later we will add server-side storage, multi-device sync and stronger security features.

  • Signup & login with a master password
  • Store site credentials (site, username, password)
  • Passwords is storred locally on browser
